Nature Photography: Understanding the Camera

This eBook will teach you how to use your camera to photograph the wonderful moments you observe in nature. It covers all types of nature photography, including landscapes, wildlife, and close-ups.




Wildflower Photography

This eBook is a complete guide to photographing wildflowers and how to post-process your images in Adobe Photoshop (CS and Elements 8). Topics covered include: equipment you’ll need, how to setup your shot, how to choose the right aperture, how to find the perfect exposure by using histograms, and the secret to getting perfect light.




53 Tips For Nature Photography

This 108-page book contains 53 of the most popular posts on my blog: PhotoNaturalist. The tips cover all types of nature photography, including landscapes, wildflife, and close-ups.




Examples: 23 Images

This 30-page book contains 23 of my photographs, along with the stories of how those images were made. The idea was inspired by my strong belief that the best way to learn something is by example. As Albert Einstein put it, "Learning by example isn’t the best way to learn. It's the only way to learn."
